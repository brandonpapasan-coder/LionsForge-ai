# Public Operations Readiness

Tracks Issue #402. This document is operational guidance, not legal advice. Public registration must remain closed until the final policies and processes are approved by the responsible business and legal owners.

## Public surfaces

- `/privacy`
- `/terms`
- `/responsible-ai`
- `/support`
- login-page links to all four surfaces

## Required launch configuration

- legal entity name and business address
- approved privacy contact and monitored support address
- policy effective dates and version identifiers
- jurisdiction-specific privacy rights and request deadlines
- subprocessors and AI-provider disclosures
- concrete retention periods for account, workspace, logs, backups, and support records
- verified account deletion and data-removal procedure
- acceptable-use and abuse escalation owner
- security-report intake and incident escalation path
- support response targets and after-hours incident handling
- age eligibility and parental-consent rules where applicable
- pricing, cancellation, refund, and subscription terms if payments are enabled
- cookie or analytics consent controls where applicable

## Data-request acceptance

Record evidence that:

1. A request can be submitted through the published channel.
2. Identity verification prevents unauthorized disclosure or deletion.
3. Account closure disables authentication.
4. Eligible workspace data is deleted or de-identified.
5. Backup and legal-hold exceptions are documented and time-limited.
6. Completion is recorded without placing private content in public issue threads.

## Public launch gate

GO requires:

- named legal and operational approvers
- final policy text with effective dates
- monitored contact channels
- tested deletion, support, abuse, and security-report workflows
- links reachable from authentication and application surfaces
- no unresolved critical or high-severity privacy, security, or support defects

Until those conditions are met, the repository pages must be treated as review drafts and not as final legal commitments.
