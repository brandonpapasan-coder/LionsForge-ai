# LionsForge AI Launch Evidence Register

Parent epic: #400

Use this register only for redacted references, timestamps, owners, outcomes, and approval status. Private operational material and tester data belong in restricted systems.

## Release provenance

Track the selected source revision, backend build, frontend build, Backend CI, Frontend CI, Security Gate, Deployment Validation, selection date, owner, and result.

## Staging acceptance

Track pass or fail evidence for:

- public service smoke checks;
- database migration;
- sign-in and session recovery;
- investigation, claim, and evidence journeys;
- validation and provenance journeys;
- education and Mentor journeys;
- provider timeout and unavailable behavior;
- backup;
- isolated recovery;
- rollback.

## Production and beta controls

Track evidence for:

- least-privilege access review;
- registration cap;
- per-user quota;
- aggregate AI budget;
- abuse control;
- maintenance mode and emergency stop;
- service outage alerts;
- database failure alert;
- elevated error alert;
- budget alert;
- production backup, recovery, and rollback.

## Policy and support

Track effective dates, approvers, and results for privacy, terms, responsible-use notice, retention, deletion, support intake, escalation, abuse reporting, and consent recording.

## Controlled beta results

Record approved targets and observed results for invited testers, active testers, critical journey pass rate, availability, latency, error rate, per-user AI cost, aggregate AI cost, support response time, severe defects, and unresolved high-impact defects.

## Incidents and defects

Use restricted records for private details. This register may link only redacted identifiers, severity, affected journey, owner, status, fix reference, and verification reference.

## Final acceptance

The final record must state whether all mandatory evidence exists, release references match, staging is accepted, production controls are accepted, policies and support are live, beta exit criteria are met, severe defects remain open, and the final decision is GO, CONDITIONAL GO, or NO-GO.

A passing repository workflow is not proof that a live environment passed acceptance.
