# LionsForge AI Market Simulator Service Layer

## Purpose
Define the execution services required for the educational paper-trading simulator.

## Portfolio Service
Responsibilities:
- Create simulated portfolios
- Calculate portfolio value
- Track cash and exposure
- Generate performance snapshots

## Trade Execution Service
Responsibilities:
- Validate simulated orders
- Apply virtual fills
- Update positions
- Record audit events

## Risk Service
Responsibilities:
- Calculate concentration risk
- Estimate volatility exposure
- Generate educational risk warnings

## Scenario Engine Service
Responsibilities:
- Load market scenarios
- Replay deterministic conditions
- Apply simulated market changes

## Research Integration
Workflow:
Research thesis -> Validation Engine -> Simulation -> Performance Review -> AI Mentor Feedback

## Safety
- Simulation only
- No brokerage execution
- No real-money trading
